§ 01 The problem

Your teams are drowning in manual tasks.

i.

Disconnected tools

CRM, ERP, communications, documents, dashboards — none of them talk. Every handoff is a person moving data around in their head.

ii.

Repetitive manual work

The same triage, the same reports, the same status updates — written by hand, every week, by people who could be doing better work.

iii.

No AI strategy

Pilots that never ship. Tools bought but never adopted. A board asking for an AI plan you don't have time to write.

Workfloow fixes this.

§ 03 The process

Four steps.

Most engagements move from audit to launch in 6–12 weeks. No 18-month transformation programmes.

01

Audit

We sit with your team for a week, shadowing workflows, reading docs, watching screens. Output: a written brief of every workflow worth automating, ranked by ROI.

Week 1 · On-site
02

Design

You get an architecture diagram, an ROI model, and a build plan you can sign off. We agree on what ships first, what comes later, what we don't build.

Weeks 2–3 · Remote
03

Build

Integrations and AI agents ship in iterations. Working demo at the end of every week. You see progress, not slides.

Weeks 3–9 · Iterative
04

Launch

Hands-on training, runbooks, and 90 days of guarded launch with our team on call. Then you own it.

Weeks 10+ · Handover
